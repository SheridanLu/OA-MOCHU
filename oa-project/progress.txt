1. ✅ 当月单项目产值占合同总价百分比报表

   - API: GET /api/reports/purchase-ratio

   - API: GET /api/reports/finance

   - 合同总额
   - 已支付金额
   - 未支付金额

   - API: GET /api/reports/payment-progress
